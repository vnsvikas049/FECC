Chemistry Assignment

Chemistry is the branch of science that deals with the properties, composition, and behaviour of matter. It is a fascinating field that explores the building blocks of a universe.

Chemical Elements

The periodic table is a tabular arrangements of chemical elements. Each element is represented by its chemical symbol, such as Fe for iron, H for hydrogen, and O for oxygen. Elements are
classified into groups and periods based on the their properties.

Chemical Equation

Chemical reactions are represented using chemical equation. Here's an example of a chemical reaction.

2H2 + O2 → 2H2O

This equation represents the reaction between hydrogen gas (H2)and oxygen gas(O2)to form water(H2O)

Important Concepts

Stoichiometry is the calculation of reactants and products in chemical reaction. It involves balancing chemical equations to ensure mass and chargeare conserved.
subscript indicates the number of atoms of each element in a molecule, while Superscript represent charges or the number of atoms in the ion.
